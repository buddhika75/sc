COVID Digital Solutions

About Us

Who we are:

COVID Digital Solutions is a collaborative initiative of the Commonwealth Center for Digital Health (CWCDH) and Commonwealth’s Systems Framework for Healthy Policy (SFHP). The vision of the CWCDH is to strategically digitalise the health systems across the components identified in the Commonwealth’s Systems Framework for Healthy Policy (SFHP) to build sustainable health systems that act as a common good for all. SFHP seeks to advance global health security and sustainable well-being for all through health system strengthening for implementation of Universal health coverage, supporting economic growth and SGDs, providing a framework for health systems assessment, planning, training, evaluation and accreditation and strengthening leadership and governance. The COVID Digital Solutions initiative is also inline with and supports the strategies laid down by the of the Interaction Council in their strategy document the Pandemic Emergency Response to the Coronavirus, COVID-19.

What is COVID Digital Solutions

The collaborative efforts for this initiative was triggered by the catastrophic effects of the COVID19 pandemic and the realisation of how digital health solutions cloud support the curtailment of such harm. Yet, it was observed that there is the lack of coordination and support for digital health solution development, deployment and sustenance, at a global scale. COVID Digital Solutions aims at creating a global platform for hosting digital health solutions giving wider visibility for possible re-use and support.

VISION

Our vision is to be the global platform to host, support proliferation and promote utilisation of digital health solutions, for management of epidemics and pandemics.

GOALS

Create and maintain a platform to host re-usable digital health solutions for management of epidemics and pandemics

Have global visibility and scope in the take-up and use of suitable digital health solutions

Share experiences, identify and promote success stories and learn from failures

Create partnerships to support for digital health projects and implementations at national, regional and global scale

Promote open source digital health solutions

Promote open data and related research
